================PBR Vintage Office 1===============
Requires Unity 5.5.0 or higher.



This pack includes 24 prefabs for creating vintage offices, other interior areas:
- modular walls (different elements);
- cabinet;
- carpet;
- chair;
- lamp;
- plant;
- chandelier;
- 2 tables;
- wall clock;
- door;
- picture frame;
- sofa pillow;
- sofa.

PBR Textures.

Day1, Day2, Night Demo scenes included.

Note: max texture size should be adjusted in texture import settings.
